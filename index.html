About Me

Hello! I’m Ayena Virna S. Arellano, a highly motivated and dedicated student currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. My academic journey initially began in Medical Laboratory Science, but as I delved deeper into the world of technology, I discovered a profound passion for IT. This realization led me to make the rewarding decision to transition into the field of Information Technology, where I could further explore the boundless opportunities and innovations that technology offers.

Throughout my academic career, I’ve developed a keen interest in various creative fields, including photography, graphic design, photo editing, video editing, and story writing. These areas of creativity have allowed me to hone my technical skills while also nurturing my artistic expression. I have always believed in the power of merging creativity with technology to create solutions that are both functional and impactful.

Beyond my studies, I take great pride in challenging myself through hands-on projects and continuous learning. I find immense satisfaction in collaborating with like-minded individuals, pushing boundaries, and working together to create meaningful and innovative work. Technology, to me, is not just a field of study, but a dynamic force that can solve complex problems and bring about positive change in the world.

In my free time, I actively engage in photography, explore new graphic design techniques, and write stories that fuel my imagination and inspire creativity. My ultimate goal is to blend my technical expertise with my creative abilities to make a lasting contribution to the digital landscape, shaping the future in ways that inspire and empower others.

As depicted in the photo above, I am the only daughter among five children in my family. I have the privilege of being the youngest of four siblings, with three older brothers who have each established their own families. This has brought a new dynamic to our family, as I am fortunate to be an aunt to two wonderful children, both a nephew and a niece. My role as the only girl in the family has shaped me in unique ways, fostering a deep sense of responsibility, adaptability, and strong family values. I cherish the close-knit relationships with my siblings and enjoy the joy and learning that comes with spending time with my young nieces and nephews.
